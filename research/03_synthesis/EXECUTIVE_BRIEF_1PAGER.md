# Executive Brief: U.S. Performance CTV Competitive Landscape (Mar 2026)

## Decision Context
U.S. CTV remains one of the fastest-growing ad channels, with IAB projecting 13.8% growth in 2026 while total U.S. ad spend is projected to grow 9.5%. Audience migration remains durable: Nielsen reported streaming at 47.5% of TV viewing in December 2025, and ad-supported viewing represented 72.9% of TV usage in Q3 2025.

## What Changed Strategically
1. The market moved from adoption to execution quality.
2. Competitive edge now depends on measurement credibility, supply-path control, and operational speed.
3. Direct-path and convergent workflows are becoming core differentiators.
4. Public-company durability is increasingly important in enterprise procurement.

## Threat Ranking (Combined)
1. **The Trade Desk**: highest structural threat through scale, enterprise agency penetration, and financial durability.
2. **Viant**: strong CTV growth mix plus direct-access supply-path expansion.
3. **MNTN**: strongest specialist in simplified performance-TV workflow with improving public financial profile.
4. **Tatari**: convergent TV and direct-path narrative are strong; lower disclosure transparency remains a diligence constraint.
5. **tvScientific/Pinterest**: high optionality; threat depends on post-acquisition integration speed and workflow depth.
6. **Vibe**: strongest in SMB/first-time TV cohorts; evidence quality is constrained by private self-reported metrics.

## Segment Read
- **SMB:** MNTN and Vibe strongest due to speed and ease-of-use.
- **Mid-market:** MNTN and Tatari both competitive; Viant gains where omnichannel integration is required.
- **Enterprise/Agency:** The Trade Desk and Viant lead on scale/governance; Tatari competitive in convergent-TV specialist evaluations.

## MNTN vs Tatari (Anchor Read)
- **MNTN advantage:** public financial transparency, operational simplicity, creative automation packaging.
- **Tatari advantage:** convergent TV operating model and stronger direct-path narrative through Upstream ecosystem.
- **Primary risk for both:** scaled DSP consolidation and rising buyer demand for externally validated measurement outcomes.

## 2027 Scenarios
1. **Measured Expansion (base case):** category keeps growing; winners combine credible measurement + low-friction execution.
2. **Consolidation:** large platforms absorb more enterprise budgets.
3. **Direct-Path Differentiation:** direct publisher rails become primary performance and efficiency battleground.

## 90-Day Priorities
- Weekly: track product/supply updates and SEC/IR disclosures.
- Monthly: refresh scorecard and segment threat ranking.
- Quarterly: publish board memo with ranking deltas and scenario probability updates.

## Immediate Actions
1. Build a recurring evidence refresh pipeline tied to the existing claim registry format.
2. Prioritize independent measurement validation to reduce dependence on vendor self-reported outcomes.
3. Track direct-path economics (before/after) across key competitors as a leading indicator of sustainable performance advantage.
